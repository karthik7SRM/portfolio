Future Enhancements

Where I am headed next — and why.

Primary Focus: AI + IoT Systems

My upcoming learning and project focus is on combining AI models with IoT-based data sources to build systems that don’t just display data, but reason about it and act on it.

Problem Areas of Interest

  • Predictive monitoring (environmental, system health, usage patterns)
  • Sensor-driven analytics with real-time decision logic
  • Early warning systems using historical + live data
  • Health, safety, or infrastructure-oriented use cases

Planned Technical Approach

Rather than treating AI and IoT as separate domains, my approach is to design a full pipeline:

Sensor Data Collection
Data Storage & Streaming
ML / Predictive Models
Alerts & Dashboards
User-Facing Decisions

Execution Philosophy

I plan to approach this the same way I handle all learning — by building real systems, validating assumptions through data, and refining designs based on failures and constraints rather than theory alone.
