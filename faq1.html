Hackathon Teammate FAQ

For teams looking to collaborate seriously.

Who should reach out?

Teams or individuals who are serious about building a complete solution — not just submitting something last minute. I work best in environments where roles, timelines, and expectations are clear.

What role do you expect in the team?

I’m comfortable joining as a Team Lead or taking up a clearly defined technical role. What matters more to me is ownership and accountability rather than title.

What domains are you open to?

I’m open to collaborating across domains — data, backend, analytics, ML-driven systems, or product-oriented builds. I adapt quickly as long as the problem statement is meaningful and execution-focused.

Availability & commitment

If I commit to a hackathon, I stay involved end-to-end. I prefer fewer collaborations with higher quality outcomes over juggling multiple teams without impact.

How can you contact me?

You can reach out directly via:

  • LinkedIn (preferred for first contact)
  • WhatsApp (for active collaborations)

I’m currently open to discussions and will respond promptly.
